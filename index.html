Pro Tips & Best Practices

Investment Analysis: Consider both immediate and long-term returns. Annualized ROI helps compare investments with different time horizons.
Marketing ROI: A healthy LTV:CAC ratio should be 3:1 or higher. ROAS of 4:1 is generally considered good for most industries.
Break-even: Aim to break even within 12 months. Higher contribution margins provide more flexibility and growth potential.
Scenario Planning: Always plan for the pessimistic scenario. If you can survive the worst case, you're in a strong position.
